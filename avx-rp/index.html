Server Rules & Guidelines

Please read and understand these rules before applying for whitelist access.

General Rules

  • No harassment, bullying, or toxic behavior towards other players
  • No cheating, exploiting bugs, or using unauthorized modifications
  • No spamming in chat or voice channels
  • No advertising other servers or services
  • Respect all staff members and their decisions
  • No inappropriate usernames or character names

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in character at all times during roleplay scenarios
  • No metagaming (using OOC information in character)
  • No powergaming (unrealistic actions or abilities)
  • Respect other players' roleplay scenarios
  • No random killing (RDM) without proper roleplay context
  • Follow realistic character development and storylines

Consequences

  • First offense: Warning and education
  • Second offense: Temporary ban (1-7 days)
  • Third offense: Extended ban (1-4 weeks)
  • Severe violations: Permanent ban
